My Favorite Fashion Trends

On this page I'll explain some of my favorite fashion trends or styles I've discovered.

Tops & Jackets


Some of my favorite recent fashion trends regarding tops are corset tops. Corset tops are great to dress up or down with sweatpants, jeans, and leather pants.


Halter tops are comfy tops that can be worn casually or dressed up with jewelry or other accessories.


Wrap tops are one of my favorite summer tops. They can be worn in several different ways depending on your style and are a great versatile top.


Abstract printed tops make your outfit stand out without having to add a lot of accessories or unique pants.


Leather blazers are a must to make your outfit classy and professional.


Cropped puffer jackets have become one of the most popular trends for the winter to stay stylish and warm.

Pants


High waisted wide leg jeans are great for elongating legs, while still being flattering and comfortable pants to go with crop tops.


Low rise pants have made their come back to the fashion world. My favorite type of low-rise jeans are ones that can be rolled over at the waist for a more relaxed look.


Leather pants are my most worn pair of pants recently in the past two years. Leather pants can be dressed up for nice dinners or worn with crewnecks and cool shoes.
